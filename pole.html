Pole, the Polar Bear

Pole, our zoo's panda bear, is the oldest and wisest of our bears. She loves gobbling up salmon.

Pole was found heavily injured on the edge of a road in Alaska, where she was taken in by wildlife rescuers. She was deemed to injured and attached to humans to return to the wild. Pole is the largest, oldest, and wisest of our bears. Polar bears are highly intelligent and playful. Two or more bears sometimes form “friendships” that last for weeks or even years. These bears wrestle as a form of play and may also feed and travel together. Individual polar bears have been observed sliding repeatedly downhill or across ice for no apparent reason other than just for the fun of it! Pole loves munching on fish and going on swims with Kim, albeit in much colder water. She is frequently seen eating dandelions.
