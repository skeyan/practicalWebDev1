Poppy, the Panda Bear

Poppy, our zoo's panda bear, is a ball of sunshine. She loves rolling down hills.

Poppy was raised here since she was a cub as a part of the Panda Bear Initiative. We are planning to welcome another panda bear to our zoo soon. She has black hip-high "stockings" extending up her hind legs. Her eye patches are oval, and a pale black band runs across the bridge of her nose. The giant panda, also known as the panda bear, is a bear native to South Central China. It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. Poppy is the cheeriest of our bears and full of energy. She can usually be found rolling down hills or snacking on bamboo that our keepers provide her. She has always been a bundle of joy and loves entertaining her siblings.
